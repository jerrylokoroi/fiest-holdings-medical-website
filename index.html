About Us

About Fiest Holdings Limited

At Fiest Holdings Limited, we help healthcare providers and medical facilities across Kenya and East Africa access reliable, high-quality medical equipment and consumables. Based in Nairobi, we serve hospitals, clinics, diagnostic laboratories, research institutions, and NGOs.

We operate as specialist sourcing agents — connecting our clients directly with verified international manufacturers and distributors, cutting out unnecessary intermediaries and ensuring competitive pricing without compromising on quality or compliance.
